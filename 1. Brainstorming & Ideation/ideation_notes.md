# Phase 1: Brainstorming & Ideation

## 🎯 Project Concept
The Voice-Based Concept Understanding Analyser (VBCUA) addresses the challenge of automated qualitative oral assessment. Traditional software checks written answers, but this platform evaluates spoken explanations for depth, precision, fluency, and semantic conceptual accuracy.

## 💡 Core Brainstorming Goals
- **Eliminate Rote Memorization**: Shift evaluations from direct keyword checks to high-dimensional contextual vector understanding.
- **Provide Multimodal Feedback**: Deliver simultaneous insights into speech delivery parameters (such as fillers and long pauses) alongside content mastery metrics.
- **Ensure Privacy and Speed**: Use cached offline local neural architectures (Whisper and SBERT) to reduce response latencies and protect student data.
